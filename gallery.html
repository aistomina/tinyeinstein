THE FACILITY AND PROGRAMS

Our brand-new, ultra modern preschool has large class rooms and a bright, sunny, outdoor play area. The Tiny Einstein Center for Child Development provides a wide variety of bilingual material, including provisions for dramatic play, math, literacy, art, science, music, kinesthetics, physical education, and gymnastics.

Our students enjoy the specialist teachers we bring in, to enrich this diverse curriculum in both languages and cultures. We strive to create an environment where the children are stimulated and excited to learn indoors and outdoors, in a space that is clean, organized, and constantly being renewed with new age appropriate materials. It is our goal to have each child leave us not only well grounded in Russian culture, but also with excellent concentration, a good sense of order, a desire to explore, and a genuine love of learning.

The academic areas are regularly renewed to maintain peak interest and excitement.
